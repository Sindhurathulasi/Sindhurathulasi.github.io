ATM is a group project done for the course User Experience, under the professor Marco Catani. The brief was to redesign the website of ATM and create a good usable user experience based on our research and user testing.

The inference from our research and interviews is that the users want an easy and a time saving purchase process. With the help of user testing, we redesigned the flow of the whole website in a way which not only helps in faster purchase process but also is user-friendly.

COLOR SCHEME

We decided to keep our color scheme in accordance with the ATM logo because we wanted it to be more relatable to the people as they were already used to the colors of ATM.
